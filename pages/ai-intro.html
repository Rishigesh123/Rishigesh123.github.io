Artificial Intelligence

The simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems.

DEFINITION

Definition

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a wide-ranging branch of computer science concerned with building smart machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include things like recognizing speech, making decisions, translating languages, and identifying patterns.

Types of AI

Artificial Intelligence is often categorized by its capabilities and functionalities:

  • Narrow AI (ANI): Designed to perform specific tasks (e.g., facial recognition, voice assistants). All current AI, including the most advanced models, falls into this category.
  • General AI (AGI): A theoretical form of AI with human-level cognitive abilities, capable of understanding, learning, and performing any intellectual task a human can.
  • Super AI (ASI): A hypothetical future AI that surpasses human intelligence and capability across all domains.
  • Generative AI: A powerful subset of AI focused on creating new, original content (text, images, audio, code) by learning patterns from existing data (e.g., ChatGPT, Midjourney).
